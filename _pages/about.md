## Introduce
Hello, I am Shinhaeng Lee, a Master’s student in the Department of Data Science at Hanyang University and a member of the Data Mining and Algorithm Lab, advised by Prof. Hyunjoon Kim. My research interests focuses on recommender systems, with a particular focus on Generative Recommendation, LLM-based sequential recommendation and personalization. I am interested in improving recommendation performance by combining user behavior signals with content features, and in developing robust models that generalize well in real-world service environments. In addition, I am also interested in approaches that leverage knowledge graphs and agents.
